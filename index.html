TITANIUM ENCODER

Easy and Free!

What is this encoder/decoder for?

The encoder process converts the text you are typing into symbols (letter by letter), while the decoder process does the opposite.

How do I use it?

Paste or type your text, then press the red button to encode or the green button to decode. The Titanium Encoder only converts alphabet letters and spaces.

Why should I use it?

Anytime you want to distort a string content so that it cannot be understood without the knowledge of the encoding patterns.
